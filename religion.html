Norway's Religion

The Christian cross

The three top religions are

  1. 68% - Lutheran Christianity
  2. 18% - Unaffiliated (atheist, agnostic, etc.)
  3. 3% - Islam

About Christianity

  • Christianity is monotheistic, meaning they only believe in one god.
  • Christianity is universalizing, it appeals to a large group of people and it is actively looking for new members.
  • It has 2.3 billion followers, and 80 million are Lutheran.
  • The holy book is The Bible.
  • Some holy sites are:
    • Jerusalem, where Jesus was crucified
    • Bethlehem, where he was born
  • The main symbol of Christianity is the cross.

Beliefs of Lutheran Christianity

  • They believe that Jesus is the son of God.
  • They believe in the trinity, that the father, the son, and the holy sprit are separate, but they are all part of God.
