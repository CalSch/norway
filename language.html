Norway's Language

Someone speaking Norwegian

How many languages are spoken?

There are 5 main languages in Norway:

  • Norwegian
    • The official language.
    • Written in 2 ways, Bokmål and Nynorsk.
  • Sámi
    • A co-official minority language
    • Is technically a language group with 3 separate languages in it.
  • Kven
    • Also a co-official minority language

Dialect

There are a lot of dialects in Norway, so here are some differences between south-east and south-west Norway:

In south-east parts of Norway, "I" is "Jé" and "They" is "Dem", but in south-west parts of Norway, "I" is "Eg" and "They" is "De".
