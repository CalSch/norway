What is Constitution Day?

Constitution day is a Norwegian holiday celebrating the signing of Norway's constitution, marking them independent from Sweden.

Is it folk or pop culture?

It is folk culture, because it is only celebrated in Norway or in Norwegian homes.

Diffusion

It diffused through relocation, people in Norway moving to other places still celebrated it.
